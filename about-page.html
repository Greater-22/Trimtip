About Us

Warm Embrace to our high esteemed Editors

Trimtip offers trimmed-slow-motion video clips from various movies, both seasonal and non seasonal movies just for the benefits of our users. Giving each video in 720p resolution but as we evolve to higher standards to push our users to their boundaries of editing, Trimtip will also improve to higher quality resolution clips. The end goal of Trimtip is to give our users a good and easier editing experience. In addition to trimmed slow-motion clips, Trimtip also offers Intro raw clips. This gives introduction clips of the different characters so as to enhance editing and giving viewers a clue of what the character is all about which also will be captivating for audiences in it's own way.
